About Sachin

Born on April 24, 1973, in Mumbai, Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is a former Indian cricketer and one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport.

International Career

Debuted in 1989 and represented India for 24 years across all formats.

Batting Style

Right-handed batsman known for his technique, footwork, and ability to dominate all bowling attacks.

Achievements

First player to score 100 international centuries and the highest run-scorer in international cricket.
